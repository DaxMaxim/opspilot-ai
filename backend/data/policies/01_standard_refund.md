# Policy: Standard Refund Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-001  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Category:** Refund  
**Priority:** Standard

## 1. Standard Refund Window

All refund requests must be submitted within **30 calendar days** of the original purchase date. Requests submitted after this window are automatically ineligible for standard processing.

## 2. Eligibility Conditions

A refund is eligible if ALL of the following conditions are met:
- The request is within the 30-day refund window
- The product/service has not been fully consumed or utilized
- The customer has a valid proof of purchase (receipt, order confirmation, or transaction ID)
- The customer has not previously received a refund for the same transaction

## 3. Refund Amounts

- **Full refund**: Issued when the product/service is returned unused or unopened within the 30-day refund window. Also applies if the product has been used for fewer than 7 days and is returned in original condition.
- **Prorated refund**: Issued when partial usage has occurred beyond 7 days but the request is still within the 30-day window (calculated based on usage percentage per POL-011)
- **No refund**: If the product/service has been fully consumed, or if the request is outside the 30-day window

## 4. Processing

- Standard refunds are processed within 3-5 business days
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method
- A confirmation email is sent upon processing

## 5. Exclusions

The following are excluded from the standard refund policy:
- Digital products after download/activation
- Custom or personalized items
- Services already rendered
- Items marked as "final sale"
