# Policy: Damaged Goods Exception

**Policy ID:** POL-002  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Category:** Exception  
**Priority:** High

## 1. Scope

This policy covers refund or replacement requests for products received in a damaged, defective, or materially different condition from what was ordered.

## 2. Extended Window

Damaged goods claims may be submitted up to **60 calendar days** from the delivery date, extending the standard 30-day refund window.

## 3. Required Documentation

The following documentation is **mandatory** for processing a damaged goods claim:
- Photographic evidence of the damage (minimum 2 photos)
- Original order confirmation or receipt
- Description of the damage or defect
- Date the damage was discovered

**Missing documentation will result in a NEED_MORE_INFO status until all items are provided.**

## 4. Verification Process

- Claims under $100: Automatic approval with valid documentation
- Claims $100-$500: Supervisor review required within 24 hours
- Claims over $500: Mandatory senior review and may require product return

## 5. Resolution Options

- Full refund to original payment method
- Replacement product shipment (if available)
- Store credit at 110% of purchase value (as goodwill gesture)

## 6. Fraud Prevention

Multiple damaged goods claims (3+ in a 12-month period) from the same customer trigger an automatic fraud review under POL-003.
