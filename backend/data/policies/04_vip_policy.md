# Policy: VIP Customer Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-004  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Category:** VIP  
**Priority:** High

## 1. VIP Tier Definitions

| Tier | Annual Spend | Benefits |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Gold | $1,000 - $4,999 | Extended refund window (45 days), priority support |
| Platinum | $5,000 - $19,999 | Extended refund window (60 days), dedicated rep, exception consideration |
| Diamond | $20,000+ | Extended refund window (90 days), dedicated rep, automatic exception approval for claims under $500 |

## 2. VIP Exception Handling

- VIP customers may request exceptions to standard policies
- Gold: Exceptions reviewed by supervisor, case-by-case basis
- Platinum: Exceptions generally approved unless fraud indicators present
- Diamond: Automatic approval for exceptions under $500, supervisor review for higher amounts

## 3. Refund Window Extensions

- VIP customers receive extended refund windows as specified in the tier table
- The extended window applies to ALL refund types (standard, damaged goods, service)
- Extensions are calculated from the purchase date, not the delivery date

## 4. Manual Review Eligibility

- All VIP customers are eligible for manual review by a senior agent
- Manual review may override standard denial decisions
- Override decisions must be documented with justification

## 5. Retention Priority

- Before denying a VIP refund, agents must consider retention alternatives:
  - Store credit at premium rate (115-125% of purchase value)
  - Product exchange with upgrade
  - Service extension at no cost
- VIP denial decisions require manager approval
