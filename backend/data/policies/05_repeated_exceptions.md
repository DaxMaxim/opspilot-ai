# Policy: Repeated Exceptions Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-005  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Category:** Exception Management  
**Priority:** Medium

## 1. Exception Limits

- Each customer is allowed a maximum of **2 policy exceptions per 12-month rolling period**
- Exceptions include: late refund approvals, policy overrides, goodwill credits
- VIP customers (Platinum and Diamond) receive up to 4 exceptions per year

## 2. Third Exception Request

When a customer submits a third exception request within 12 months:
- The request is automatically flagged for senior review
- Standard recommendation: **DENY** with explanation of exception limit
- The system must reference the prior two exceptions in the decision rationale

## 3. Pattern Assessment

The system evaluates the pattern of exception requests:
- **Legitimate pattern**: Genuine issues across different transactions → consider approval
- **Gaming pattern**: Repeated similar requests exploiting policy gaps → deny and flag
- **Escalation pattern**: Increasingly aggressive repeat requests → escalate to manager

## 4. Escalation Criteria

Escalate to management if:
- Customer is VIP and has exceeded their exception limit
- Customer threatens legal action or regulatory complaint
- Pattern analysis indicates potential policy abuse
- The aggregate value of exceptions exceeds $1,000

## 5. Documentation

All exception decisions must include:
- Reference to previous exceptions (dates, amounts, reasons)
- Justification for approval or denial
- Any customer retention measures considered
