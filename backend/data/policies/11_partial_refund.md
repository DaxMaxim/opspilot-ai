# Policy: Partial Refund Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-011  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Category:** Refund  
**Priority:** Standard

## 1. Partial Refund Eligibility

A partial refund may be issued when:
- The product/service has been partially used or consumed
- The customer is outside the full refund window but within the partial refund window (31-60 days)
- Only a portion of a bundled purchase is being returned
- The product has minor defects that reduce but don't eliminate its value

## 2. Calculation Rules

### Time-Based Proration
- Days 1-30: Full refund eligible for unused items (per POL-001). Prorated refund if partially used.
- Days 31-45: 75% refund
- Days 46-60: 50% refund
- Days 61+: No refund (except under exception policies)

### Usage-Based Proration
- 0-25% usage: 90% refund
- 26-50% usage: 70% refund
- 51-75% usage: 40% refund
- 76-100% usage: No refund

### Bundle Proration
- Individual item value is calculated based on the bundle price allocation
- Returned items are refunded at their allocated value minus a 10% restocking fee

## 3. Minimum Refund Threshold

- Partial refunds under $5.00 are not processed
- Instead, a store credit for the full item value may be offered

## 4. Customer Communication

- Partial refund calculations must be clearly explained to the customer
- Breakdown of the calculation must be included in the decision
