# Policy: High-Value Transaction Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-008  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Category:** High-Value  
**Priority:** High

## 1. Definition

A high-value transaction is any single transaction with a total value of **$500 or more**.

## 2. Mandatory Requirements

All refund requests for high-value transactions require:
- **Senior review**: Must be reviewed by a Team Lead or above
- **Enhanced verification**: Additional identity verification of the customer
- **Documentation**: Complete documentation with no exceptions
- **Dual approval**: Two authorized personnel must approve the refund

## 3. Tiered Review Process

| Transaction Value | Review Level | Additional Requirements |
|------------------|-------------|----------------------|
| $500 - $999 | Team Lead | Standard enhanced review |
| $1,000 - $4,999 | Manager | Written justification required |
| $5,000 - $9,999 | Director | Executive summary required |
| $10,000+ | VP/C-Suite | Board notification required |

## 4. Fraud Risk Assessment

High-value transactions receive automatic fraud risk scoring:
- Low risk (score < 30): Proceed with standard high-value review
- Medium risk (score 30-70): Enhanced investigation required
- High risk (score > 70): Automatic hold and fraud team notification

## 5. Refund Method

- High-value refunds are processed via wire transfer or original payment method only
- No store credit option for amounts over $1,000
- Processing time: 5-10 business days (extended from standard 3-5 days)

## 6. Audit Trail

All high-value refund decisions are logged in the compliance audit system with:
- Full decision rationale
- All reviewer approvals
- Risk assessment results
- Complete customer communication history
