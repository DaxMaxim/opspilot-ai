# Policy: Manual Override Rules

**Policy ID:** POL-010  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Category:** Override  
**Priority:** High

## 1. Override Authorization

Manual overrides allow authorized personnel to approve requests that would normally be denied by standard policy. Only the following roles may initiate overrides:

| Role | Override Limit | Requires Counter-Approval |
|------|---------------|--------------------------|
| Team Lead | Up to $500 | No |
| Manager | Up to $2,000 | No |
| Director | Up to $10,000 | Manager counter-approval |
| VP | Unlimited | Director counter-approval |

## 2. Valid Override Reasons

An override may be granted for:
- Customer retention of high-lifetime-value accounts
- Goodwill gesture for documented service failures
- Policy gap where no existing policy adequately covers the situation
- Legal or regulatory compliance requirement
- Executive directive

## 3. Override Documentation Requirements

Every manual override must include:
- Override reason (selected from approved list)
- Written justification (minimum 50 words)
- Reference to the policy being overridden
- Expected business impact assessment
- Customer communication plan

## 4. Audit Trail

- All overrides are logged permanently in the compliance system
- Monthly override reports are generated for management review
- Override frequency by agent/manager is tracked
- Excessive overrides (>5 per month per individual) trigger a process review

## 5. Restrictions

Manual overrides are NOT permitted for:
- Cases with confirmed fraud indicators
- Cases where documentation has been falsified
- Cases already escalated to legal
- Cases involving regulatory violations
