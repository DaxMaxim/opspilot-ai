# Policy: Service Credit Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-012  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Category:** Alternative Resolution  
**Priority:** Standard

## 1. Service Credit as Alternative

Service credits may be offered as an alternative to monetary refunds when:
- The customer prefers continued use of the service
- A monetary refund is not eligible but goodwill gesture is warranted
- As a retention tool for valued customers
- When partial service disruption occurred but does not qualify for refund

## 2. Credit Amounts

| Scenario | Credit Amount |
|----------|-------------|
| Minor service disruption (< 4 hours) | 10% of monthly fee |
| Moderate service disruption (4-24 hours) | 25% of monthly fee |
| Major service disruption (> 24 hours) | 50% of monthly fee |
| Complete service failure | 100% of monthly fee |
| Goodwill gesture (no fault) | 15% of transaction value |
| Retention offer (VIP) | 20-30% of annual spend |

## 3. Credit Terms

- Service credits are applied to the next billing cycle
- Credits expire after 12 months if unused
- Credits are non-transferable and non-cashable
- Maximum credit accumulation: 100% of one month's billing

## 4. Eligibility

Service credits are NOT available for:
- Products (only applicable to subscription/service accounts)
- Accounts with outstanding balance
- Accounts flagged for fraud
- Cases where a monetary refund has already been issued

## 5. Approval Authority

- Credits under $50: Automatic approval
- Credits $50-$200: Supervisor approval
- Credits over $200: Manager approval
- Retention credits: Must be approved by retention team lead
